About C/I

INSPIRING THE NEXT GENERATION OF DIVERSE TECHNOLOGY LEADERS.

C/I exists to cultivate the potential of America’s underserved youth by narrowing the technical skills gap in our economies and the opportunity gap in our communities.

C/I Code Camp

Over the course of this all day event students will...

  • Identify and brainstorm tech solutions to health problems
  • Attend workshops from volunteer professionals
  • Learn valuable research and product validation skills
  • Create and present their original web or mobile projects
  • Take home awesome prizes and swag
  • Have Fun!
